# Brand Guidelines

## Brand Positioning
Revvel Forensic Studio: trustworthy, precision-first forensic intelligence for modern media.

## Voice
- Clear
- Evidence-oriented
- Professional
- Actionable

## Messaging Pillars
1. **Clarity from complexity** — recover signal from noisy imagery.
2. **Operational speed** — reduce analyst turnaround time.
3. **Defensible outputs** — workflows designed for auditability.

## Visual Direction
- Dark, high-contrast interface for investigative contexts.
- Accent tones for evidence confidence states (info/warn/high-risk).
- Consistent iconography for enhancement vs forensic actions.

## Naming Conventions
- Keep product references consistent: "Revvel Forensic Studio".
- Use "Website in Test" label for the Vercel demo environment.
